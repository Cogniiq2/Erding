- Centers and certificates: center names come from `official-centers`; certification statements and quality reports come from `official-certificates` and `official-quality-reports`.
- Gynaecology and birth: service names, consultation types and birth-event framing come from `official-gyn` and `official-birth-events`.
- Nursing: nursing leadership and professional profile come from `official-care`.
- Careers and education: job listings link to `career-platform`; employer profile and education framing are supported by `official-about` and `official-care`.
- Leadership: current names and roles come from `official-leadership` and the imprint lines for legal roles.
- Mission and history: content domains map to `official-mission` and `official-history`.
- Legal pages: imprint entries come from `official-impressum`; privacy text inventories the current site build and references `official-privacy` for the responsible contact.
- Accessibility: the page describes implemented frontend behavior only and avoids a full compliance claim until a specialist audit is complete.
